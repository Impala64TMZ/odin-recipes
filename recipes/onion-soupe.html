Onion Soupe

Description

This is the original French onion soupe recipe.


Prep time : 15 mins Cook time : 40 mins Total time : 55 mins
Servings : 4


Ingredients

  • 1/2 cups unsalted butter
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cups sliced oinons
  • 5 cups beef broth
  • 2 tbsp dry sherry
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 pinch salt and pepper
  • 4 slices French bread
  • 4 slices provolone cheese
  • 2 slices Swiss cheese, diced
  • 1/4 grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Melt butter with olive oil in a 8-quart stock pot over medium heat. Add onions and continually stir until tender translucent. Do not brown the onions.
  2. Ass beef broth, sherry and thyme. Season with salt and pepper. Let simmer for 30 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, preheat the oven's broiler.
  4. Ladle soup into oven-safe serving bowls and place one slice of bread on top of each (bread may be broken into pieces if you prefer). Layer each slice of bread with a slice of provolon, 1/2 slice diced Swiss and 1tbsp Parmesan cheese.
  5. Place bowls on a cookie sheet and broil in the preheated oven until cheese bubbles and browns slightly, 2 to 3 minutes.
